Georg Wachter

Team Lead

Harry Potter, Is that you?? No. It’s Georg Wachter (a.k.a master axethrower). His uncanny resemblance to Harry Potter does, however, explain his determination to persevere through unmatched hardship such as de-bugging the website. From wet lab experiments to dry lab computer design, Georg passionately immerses himself into all facets of the project. How does he do it? He’s actually a wizard.

Alberto Scarampi

Wet Lab Lead

Who needs a rotary evaporator when you have a rotary Albi-vaporator? Albi is your stereotypical mad scientist who exerts as much passionate devotion to making liposomes as he does to making gelato. If you have any questions about cyanobacteria or stochasticity, just ask him. He’ll be ‘glad you asked’. Although he stays humble about his unmatched brain power, he likes to boast about how he knows ‘the best pizza restaurant in London’. Still a matter of debate…

Alisdair Stevenson

Dry Lab Lead

Alisdair's job was already difficult enough without having the extra headache of having to explain biology to a physicist. Nevertheless, he went about this job with the same enthusiasm and determination that he showed when campaigning alongside Hansa for margherita pizza. The dry lab would have been lost without his leadership and his dry humour only proved further that he was a natural for this job.

Kevin Vela

Wet Lab

Who likes to assemble liposomes engineered with DNA-nanopores whilst listening to latin music? This guy. Also known as plate reader sensei, master of liposome extrusion and creator of the nanoDIPs spotify playlist, Kevin demonstrates a meticulous but lively work ethic in the lab and brings energy to the team. Favourite controversial topic of discussion? Arguing with albi about the best pizza restaurant in London.

Sonia Shyfrin

Wet Lab

This one is breaking the synthetic ceiling for freshers across Imperial College. A quick learner, Sonia's love for matcha is matched by her infectious energy. She stirs serendipity - be it resulting, randomly, in our best ever liposomes or meeting a sought-after expert. Sonia is also our resident sketchist and instagrammer - watch out, you may feature in her webcomic!
